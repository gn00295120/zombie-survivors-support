# Vigilmort — Support

**Vigilmort** is an offline, single-player chibi fantasy tower-defense game for
iPhone and iPad.

## Contact

For questions or bug reports, email
**hackerone.lucasfutures@gmail.com**. Please include your device model, iOS version,
game version, and a short description of what happened.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the game need an internet connection?

No. The complete campaign is playable offline. The Privacy Policy and Support
buttons open these public pages in your system browser and therefore require a
connection only when you choose to use them.

### Where is my campaign stored?

Campaign progress and settings are stored only on your device. Deleting the app also
deletes its local progress. The game has no account or cloud-save service.

### How do I start over?

Choose **New Campaign** on the main menu and confirm the reset. Your current stage
stars and unlocks will be replaced with a fresh campaign.

### The game was interrupted. Did I lose the battle?

Returning from the Home screen or another interruption keeps the battle paused until
you explicitly choose **Resume**.

### Does the game contain ads or in-app purchases?

No. Version 1.0 contains no advertising, tracking, or in-app purchases.
