## FAQ
**Q: Does the game need an internet connection?**
No. The entire game is playable offline. A connection is only used by the App Store when making or restoring purchases.

**Q: I reinstalled the app — where are my purchases?**
Open Store ▸ Restore Purchases (requires a one-time internet connection so the App Store can replay your purchases).

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
Email us with your device model, iOS version, and a short description.
