# Zombie Survivors — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 3, 2026_

**Zombie Survivors** ("the app") is an offline single-player game published by Lucas Futures.

## Data Collection
The app collects **no personal data**. Period.

- No analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, no tracking.
- No account, no registration, no server of ours — game progress, scores, and settings are stored **only on your device**.
- The app makes no network requests of its own.

## In-App Purchases
Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. Purchase entitlements are recorded locally on your device so they keep working offline.

## Children's Privacy
The app collects no data from anyone, including children.
