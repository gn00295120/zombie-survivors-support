# Last Outpost — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 16, 2026_

**Last Outpost** ("the app") is an offline, single-player game published by Lucas
Futures.

## Data collection

The app does **not collect personal data**.

- No analytics, advertising, attribution, or tracking SDKs are included.
- No account, registration, server, or cloud-save service is used.
- Campaign progress and settings are stored only on your device.
- The app does not make its own network requests.
- No data is linked to you or used to track you across apps or websites.

## External links

The in-game Privacy Policy and Support buttons can ask iOS to open these public pages
in your system browser. Visiting them is optional and is governed by your browser and
the hosting provider's policies. The game itself does not receive browsing data.

## Children's privacy

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Any future change to this policy will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
